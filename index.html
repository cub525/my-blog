Galatians 3 Blog Post

For a Priests activity, my quorum went to a synagogue. We saw the Torah and talked about Jewish history. Across from the Torah there was a 10-foot bookshelf, filled with books. The Rabbi said that those books comprised the Oral Law, and that he had studied these books for years. One of the reasons the Pharisees did not like the teachings of Jesus was that he did not teach or follow the oral law, which invalidated many of their life studies and threatened their position of power. One of these laws says that any man hung from a tree is accursed of God, and the Pharisees used this to prove that Jesus could not have been the Messiah, as the Messiah would not have been accursed of God. Paul, in his ministry, described Jesus as having been accursed for our sake. In essence Paul said that this section of the Oral law summarized the Condescension of Jesus, and that by doing so He had saved us all.
